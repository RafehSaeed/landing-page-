Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. What We Collect

Bemi collects only the information needed to create meaningful matches:

  • Profile information — name, date of birth, gender, orientation, photos, bio, interests, and dealbreakers
  • Location data — city and country for discovery and city-unlock features
  • Device information — for push notifications, app performance, and security
  • Usage data — swipe behavior, match history, and chat interactions to maintain the slot system

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate the Bemi platform:

  • Match you with compatible users based on preferences and location
  • Enforce the 6-match limit and swap accountability system
  • Power the city-unlock viral growth mechanic
  • Send notifications about matches, messages, and city unlock status

3. What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for targeted advertising outside the app
  • We do not create fake profiles or inflate user counts
  • We do not share your precise location — only city-level data

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on encrypted servers. Photos are processed and stored via Backblaze B2 with access controls. We use industry-standard security practices including JWT authentication and TLS encryption for all communications.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Update your profile information in-app
  • Toggle push notification preferences
  • Export your data by contacting us via our Contact page

6. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, reach out via our Contact page.
